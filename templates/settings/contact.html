Help Center

Find quick answers to the most common AFI Connect questions. Choose a topic to see the FAQs for that subject.

How do I update my policy information?

From the Policy Overview page select Edit Policy. You can update contact details, addresses, and coverage options. Changes take effect once you confirm and sign electronically.

Can I add a new driver online?

Yes. Go to Drivers within your policy, choose Add Driver, and complete the required details. You will receive a confirmation email once the driver is approved.

Where can I download my policy documents?

Open the Documents tab on your policy dashboard and select the PDF you need. Most policy documents are available to download immediately after your policy is issued.

How do I start a new claim?

Select File a Claim from the Claims hub and follow the guided steps. You can save progress at any point and return later from your dashboard.

What information should I have ready?

Have the incident date, location, and any photos or police reports available. Uploading documents during your submission helps our team begin the review sooner.

Where can I track my claim status?

Claim updates are posted on the My Claims page. You will also receive email notifications when we request more details or when a claim stage changes.

How do I change my payment method?

Visit Billing & Payments, choose Payment Methods, and add a new card or bank account. Set the new method as default to use it for future payments.

When are automatic payments processed?

AutoPay runs at 8:00 AM CST on your due date. If the due date falls on a weekend or holiday, the payment processes on the next business day.

Can I see past statements?

Yes. In Billing History, select any month to download or print the statement. The past 24 months of statements are stored automatically.
